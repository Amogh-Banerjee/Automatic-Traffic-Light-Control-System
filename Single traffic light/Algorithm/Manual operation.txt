Manual Operation
1. While performing step 5 of Automatic Operation, if some data is received at the
   serial port then switch to Manual Operation.
2. Clear P2.0, P2.1 and P2.2
3. Check the data that has been received.
   If the received character is 'R' 
	Go back to step 3 of automatic operation.
   If it is 'O'
	Go back to step 6 of automatic operation.
   If it is 'G'
	Go back to step 8 of automatic operation.
